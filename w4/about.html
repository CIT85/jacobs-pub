My Understanding of HTTPbin

HTTPbin is essentially a free service to test a form and view its data.

When a form is sent to HTTPbin it sends the data back to the client as a sort of organized info dump providing fields and their responses along with other information such as client ip.

the get method stores all form information in the form in the url whereas push stores it as a packet

this makes push more secure but the get method allows a form to be bookmarked though I don't know why you would want that anyways.
