My understanding of the boxmodel

The above boxmodel is what I am applying to all images across the site except this one. It has no padding as it doesn't make since for my usecase in my option. Then there is a 3px border to give contrast between the image border and the site's background. Then a double outline in a light blue color to sort of blend the outside border into the background. Finally a margin of 0.75rem to give some space around the image.
